About TopSpinUSA

We are a team of players, fitters and product specialists who curate the best tennis gear so you can focus on your game.

Our Mission

TopSpinUSA exists to make quality tennis equipment accessible and understandable. We test gear, simplify choices, and provide honest product guidance so players can buy with confidence.

How we work

  1. Curate products from trusted, performance-driven brands.
  2. Provide clear specs, tests and size guidance.
  3. Offer fast shipping and thoughtful returns.

Our values

  • Player-first product curation
  • Honesty in reviews and fit
  • Sustainable and responsible sourcing

Our Story

Founded by passionate players, TopSpinUSA began as a small online shop and evolved into a curated destination for serious players. Along the way we learned the importance of fit, balance and coaching advice — and we keep that in mind in every product we recommend.

Milestones

  • 2016 — Founded by former pro-player Elliot
  • 2018 — Introduced in-house racket demos
  • 2022 — Expanded fitting resources and size guides
